to my younger self: a letter

If i could say something to my younger self, it would be: live. 

Just that, that's enough statement to accomplish the ultimate goal in life, that would make me find the ultimate truths, than anything else.

By really living, one realize that getting angry, emotional, sad, raged, and all those bad feelings, never leds one to some truth, instead just apart one from it.

By truly living, I embrace my soul, my truer self. I end up getting everything I always wanted, a fulfilled life. 

I would like to get to the end of my life with the satisfaction that I lived for the good, with the satisfaction that I truly lived, and didn't waste my most precious gift that God has gave me.

By truly living, one focus all his strengths, his attention, his entire soul in whatever one wants. One end up putting all his efforts in the things that matters the most, if one ignore everything else and just praise life.

My body claims for that significance that only life gives.

And I think that that's what Jesus meant with being reborn.

To me the act of reborn, means to experience life, in a so different way, that it just feel like one is living in a whole new life, a better one.

One starts opens ones eyes and noticing truth everywhere, because now one has become the truth, and everyone who have seeing God, has seeing truth and viceversa.

It's evidently that appreciating life and embracing the soul, one knows God, because God is life, and he is the one who gives life.
